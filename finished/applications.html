What is MR? History Technology Applications

Mixed Reality Applications

MR has the potential to change environments big and small. Although the extent that this technology will shift our world is still unknown, developers are exploring new application possibilities for mixed reality every day. Roll over Click the images below for more information:

Workplace

By wearing MR, workers could get the information they need at the exact moment they need it. MR could revolutionize the hospitals, factories, and even offices, allowing for immediate data sharing.

Architecture

With MR, architects could manipulate structure, measurments, and style at the wave of a finger. The new technology can allow for for accurate drafting and faster and safer building.

Archaeology

The intricacies of archaeological artifacts are often lost when displayed by flat images. MR technology could create a lifelike, interactive display of findings and enable researchers to study them more thoroughly.

Education

With the rising popularity of marrying technology and learning in the classroom, MR could offer students a variety of new learning experiences, including interactive math games, mess-free science experiments, and more.
